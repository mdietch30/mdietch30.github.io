About Me

I am a Sophomore from Orange, CT. I am a Marketing major at the University of Maine. Check out the school website in the link below titled, "UMaine". For my entire life, until I started school at UMaine, I played baseball in the spring, summer and fall. My family is the definition of a baseball family so it has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I was a catcher, outfielder and occasionally pitched. I attended Amity Regional High School where I played baseball for all four years. I was also on the ski team at my school for all four years which included being a captain my senior year. I lettered in both sports. Since I have stopped playing baseball, I started coaching AAU travel ball. It has been a lot of fun and I enjoyed working with the kids, passing my knowledge on to them, and watching them grow throughout the summer.
